SIGN GO — WEBSITE
=================

HOW TO VIEW
-----------
Double-click "index.html" to open the site in your web browser.

ADD YOUR LOGO (important — 1 step)
----------------------------------
1. Save your Sign Go logo image as a file named:  logo.png
2. Put it inside the "assets" folder (Desktop/SignGo/assets/logo.png)
3. Refresh the page.

Until you do this, the site shows a "SIGN GO" text logo automatically,
so nothing looks broken.

WHAT'S INCLUDED
---------------
index.html        - the full website (all sections)
css/styles.css    - all styling / colors (matched to your logo)
js/main.js        - menu, photo loading, and the quote form
assets/           - put your logo.png here
