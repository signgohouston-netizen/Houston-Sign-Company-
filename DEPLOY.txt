PUTTING SIGN GO ONLINE
======================

EASIEST METHOD — Netlify Drop (5 minutes, free, no coding)
----------------------------------------------------------
1. Go to:  https://app.netlify.com/drop
2. Drag the WHOLE "SignGo" folder onto the page.
3. Wait a few seconds — your site is live at a free address like
   https://random-name.netlify.app
4. (Optional) In Site settings, click "Change site name" to make it
   something like  signgotx.netlify.app
5. (Optional) Add your real domain (signgotx.com) under
   Domain settings → Add a domain.

THE QUOTE FORM WILL WORK AUTOMATICALLY on Netlify:
- Submissions appear in your Netlify dashboard under "Forms".
- To also get an email for each one: Site settings → Forms →
  Form notifications → Add email notification → Info@signgotx.com

PREFER FORMSPREE INSTEAD? (works on any host)
---------------------------------------------
1. Sign up free at  https://formspree.io  and create a form that
   sends to  Info@signgotx.com
2. Copy the form ID (the code after /f/ in your endpoint URL).
3. Open  js/main.js , find the line:   var FORMSPREE_ID = '';
   and put your ID inside the quotes, e.g.  var FORMSPREE_ID = 'xmyzabcd';
4. Re-upload the site.

OTHER FREE HOSTS
----------------
- Vercel:        https://vercel.com  (drag/drop or connect a folder)
- GitHub Pages:  push this folder to a repo, enable Pages
- Cloudflare Pages: https://pages.cloudflare.com

CONNECTING YOUR DOMAIN (signgotx.com)
-------------------------------------
After deploying, your host will give you DNS records (or nameservers)
to enter at wherever you bought the domain (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.).
The host walks you through it — usually just changing the nameservers.
